Science

I enjoy studying science because it gives us an opportunity to learn about ourselves. Mostly, I study physics (quantum mechanics, astrophysics), further maths and chemistry. However, I find all areas of science interesting. I have 3-hour sessions with my tutor 3-4 times a week. Sometimes, I go to lectures hosted by an education centre where invited scientists give lectures about different areas of science. Studying science has enabled me to understand the world how it is and look at everything critically.
